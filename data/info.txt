──────────────────────────────
1. About AMTICS
──────────────────────────────
The Asha M. Tarsadia Institute of Computer Science and Technology (AMTICS) is a constituent college of Uka Tarsadia University, located on the Maliba Campus in South Gujarat.

Established: 2021  
AMTICS was established in the Year 2021
The previous Director of AMTICS was : Dr. Dharmendra Bhatti
The Director of AMTICS is : Dr. Vishvajit Bakrola
Focus: Innovation and excellence in Computer Science and Engineering  
Working Hours: 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM (Monday – Saturday)  
Minimum Attendance Criteria: 75%  

Programs / Courses Offered:  
- Undergraduate (7) and Postgraduate (1) programs in emerging fields:  
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning  
  • Cloud Computing  
  • Cyber Security  
  • Software Engineering  
  • Bioscience

9. Code of Conduct for Students
──────────────────────────────
1. Uphold dignity of AMTICS.  
2. Set and pursue high academic goals.  
3. Attend daily prayer at 8:25 AM.  
4. Be polite and respectful.  
5. Maintain mandatory attendance (medical certificate required for absences).  
6. Balance academics with extracurriculars.  
7. Wear uniform and ID card.  
8. Submit exam forms on time.  
9. Follow all institute & university rules.  
10. Do not form organizations/collect funds without permission.  
11. Substance use → expulsion.  
12. Ragging is prohibited.  
13. Damaging property → fines.  
14. No illegal or disorderly activities.  
15. Maintain dignity on social media.  
16. Consult counselors for academic/personal issues.
